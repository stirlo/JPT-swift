How to use this converter:

  1. Visit the Chabad.org Zmanim page.
  2. Enter your location and select the correct option from the dropdown.
  3. On the results page, click either:
    • "Monthly view" to see a table of Zmanim for the entire month, or
    • "RSS" icon (usually at the bottom of the page) to view the RSS feed.
  4. Copy the entire contents of the monthly view table or the RSS feed.
  5. Paste the copied content into the text box below.
  6. Select your preferred language and click "Convert to ICS".
  7. Download the generated ICS file to import into your calendar application.
